Our Story

Redefining Street Food Since 2018

Urban Bites started with a simple mission: to serve restaurant-quality food in a mobile format. Our founder, Chef Emily Chen, left her position at a Michelin-starred restaurant to bring gourmet flavors to the streets.

We source our ingredients from local farms and suppliers, ensuring that everything we serve is fresh and sustainable. Our team of talented chefs constantly experiments with new flavors and techniques to create unforgettable culinary experiences.
